About Me

I’m a learning experience designer and product-minded professional passionate about building smart, scalable solutions that help people learn, grow, and succeed.

With a background spanning instructional design, product strategy, and solutions enablement, I work at the intersection of education, technology, and business. My experience includes leading national certification programs, designing engaging and measurable learning experiences, developing technical onboarding resources, and partnering with engineering, product, and sales teams to turn ideas into impactful outcomes.

What drives me most is a deep curiosity and eagerness to learn. Whether diving into new technologies, analyzing product feedback, or exploring better ways to support customers and learners, I bring a strong capacity to learn quickly and connect the dots across disciplines. My blend of technical fluency and learning design expertise allows me to bridge gaps between teams and keep the user experience at the center.

I hold advanced degrees in education, mathematics, and computer science education, and bring years of experience designing and scaling solutions that not only improve learning but also drive adoption, engagement, and business results.

Known for simplifying complex topics and translating them into actionable learning strategies, I empower adult learners and teams to achieve measurable success. I also lead AI-driven initiatives that enhance instructional design and streamline internal processes, helping organizations scale their learning programs more effectively.

As a self-taught programmer with a passion for continuous growth, I bring curiosity, adaptability, and innovation to every learning solution I create. My work has directly impacted over 500+ educators and professionals, delivering engaging, results-oriented learning experiences that make a lasting impact.
